## How to cite (rules)

- **Numbers verbatim** — every metric below comes from the page; don't round up or extrapolate.
- **3 studies are anonymous** (L.A. fashion brand, men's fashion brand, Shopify agency) — describe them ("a nine-figure L.A. fashion brand"), never invent a name.
- **Named voices** (the `Voice:` field) are quotable in decks — e.g. Cody Plofker (CEO, Jones Road) carries weight with founders.
- **Negative-result studies are de-risking proof**, not growth wins: Joseph Joseph (avoided scaling a bad channel), WillPowders & The Feed (cut wasted brand-search spend), Konges Sløjd (found true CAC was *worse* than benchmark). Cite them as "Polar tells you the truth even when it hurts" — powerful with skeptics and finance.
- **Known discrepancies:** Razor Group page title says 40+ brands, body says 70+; Cube Partnership's header repeats Yellow Pop's −36%/+28% numbers not substantiated in its body — cite only body numbers.
- Recheck the source page monthly; metrics and studies rotate.
